Description

Charge and display your PlayStation Portal™ remote player next to your PlayStation®5 console with the officially licensed PowerA Charging Station for PlayStation Portal™ Remote Player with Lumectra™.

  • Charge and Display – Charge the PlayStation Portal™ remote player and display it next to your PlayStation®5 console
  • RGB LED Indicator – Built-in RGB charging indicator with touch capacitive button to change lighting modes. Modes - Standard, Off, Cycle, Wave, Rainbow Breathing
  • Weighted Base – Added stability to keep the Charging Station in place
  • Effortless Recharging – Easily slides into place
  • USB Powered – Includes a 5 ft./1.5 m USB-C® to USB-C cable and magnetic USB-C charging adapter
  • Officially licensed by PlayStation®
  • *PlayStation Portal™ Remote Player and PlayStation®5 console not included
