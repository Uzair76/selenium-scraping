Description

Vibrant LED Lighting

Change the look of your controller any time with this innovative light-up design. Customize the hue and the brightness for three distinct sections of the controller. Select your colors to match your mood, your style, or your other light-up gaming gear. Coordinate or contrast colors across the three zones and enjoy a seriously electric gaming experience.

Advanced Gaming Buttons

Keep your thumbs on the precision-tuned analog sticks, while mapping actions to two buttons on the back of the controller. Just hold the program button for two seconds, select the gaming button you want to assign and simply choose either the right or left Advanced Gaming Button. Achieve maximum efficient gameplay on-the-fly, and stay in control to keep on crushing it.

Total Gaming Immersion

Dual-rumble motors help take your gaming experience to the next level, providing tactile feedback and sensations that bring your virtual worlds to life. You'll love the range of feedback, from nuanced waves to lengthy, deep quakes.

Three-Way Trigger Locks

Featuring 3-way locks for each trigger, the Spectra Infinity lets you finetune your controller to your performance needs, in an instant. Quickly adjust for shorter trigger throws and faster action or go full trigger depth to suit your game and your opponents.

Packed with Features

The Spectra Infinity Enhanced Wired Controller for Xbox Series X|S has everything you need. Featuring the Xbox Share Button, a Volume Dial with one-touch Mic Mute, two embedded anti-friction rings and a 3.5mm audio jack, you'll enjoy a complete gaming experience. Crystal-clear stereo sound and intuitive controls make in-game communication a breeze. Play from anywhere in the room with the braided 10-foot cable featuring a snap-lock connector and inline release.
