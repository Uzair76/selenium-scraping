Description

The Epson WorkForce DS-900WN is a high-speed, Wi-Fi and network-integrated scanner with open platform compatibility. Paper alignment plate included. This business scanner offers a comprehensive solution for efficient, reliable, secure and user-friendly document scanning. With its advance features, seamless integration (with third party solutions) and an intuitive interface, the DS-900WN is ideal for businesses of all sizes.

DESIGNED FOR BUSINESS EFFICIENCY

Open platform

The DS-900WN features web-based open platform which allows seamless integration with Epson software or third-party software solutions. This means that businesses can customise their systems and integrate the scanner with existing infrastructures, business processes and workflows.

Network connectivity

Integrated network connectivity means that the scanner can be shared by multiple users and each user is able to create their own settings. Also, the LCD touchscreen allows the user to operate the scanner directly, without the use of a PC.

High speed

The DS-900WN processes and digitises a high volume of documents, quickly and securely. Scan speed of 70ppm/140ipm, a daily duty cycle of 11,000 pages and the included paper alignment plate, make this an ideal scanner for a wide range of industry sectors: from government, healthcare and legal, to finance, accounting and education.
