Description

Canon's DR-C240 scanner is engineered for businesses that need to scan documents of diverse size and material in high volume. User friendly, this surprisingly affordable scanner works at 45ppm/90ipm to effortlessly capture up to 4,000 scans a day.

Easy operation

Every task is easy with automatic page size and text direction detection, text enhancement, a deskew function and automatic colour detection, plus a user-friendly control panel.

Maximise productivity

Large feed rollers work individually, to reduce instances of paper skewing during the feed process. This is a benefit when using smaller sheets of paper or scanning mixed sizes together.

Minimise downtime

Get the job started fast with Ultrasonic Double Feed Detection and Double Feed Release (DFR) and enjoy best in class reliability even with ultra-thin papers from 27gsm.

Responsive to your needs

Robust in construction, this business scanner takes in up to 60 pages at a time and flies at 45ppm/90ipm to effortlessly capture up to 4,000 scans a day.

Features
  • Sheetfed scanner is a good choice for scanning large documents
  • The colour scanner enables scanning the document or photo in its original colour
  • Colour accuracy can be managed with 24-bit depth
