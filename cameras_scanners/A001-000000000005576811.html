Description

The network enabled document scanner that's the intelligent choice

The Epson WorkForce DS-790WN is the most intelligent introduction into Epson's scanner portfolio yet. This high speed sheet-fed scanner is bundled with features that will increase efficiency and productivity in the workplace.

KEY FEATURES & BENEFITS

  • Exceptional speed - 45 ppm / 90 ipm1 scan speeds for fast and productive scanning means that documents can be stored and or shared efficiently.
  • Intuitive colour LCD touchscreen - allows easy job creation without the need to access a computer.
  • PC-free scanning for easy sharing and collaboration
  • Scan to searchable PDF's - scan and edit documents; save scanned documents as PDF files. The included OCR software included means that documents can be scanned and saved as text-searchable PDF files, dramatically improving the ability to search and successfully locate information contained in scanned and stored documents.
  • Versatile connectivity with LAN and wireless networking and USB 3.0 connectivity.
  • Versatile paper handling - the 100 sheet ADF will scan stacks business cards and extra-long paper.
  • Connect to document management software - TWAIN and ISIS drivers for seamless integration between the scanner and custom built business applications
  • Powerful duty cycle - Scan up to 7,000 sheets per day.
Features
  • Large format scanner - Helps improve access to and management of all your large files
  • The colour scanner enables scanning the document or photo in its original colour
  • 30-bit of colour depth ensures accurate colour reproduction with fine detail
