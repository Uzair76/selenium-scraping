Description
Step up to DSLR photography

Designed for first-time DSLR owners who want impressive results straight out of the box, capture those magic moments no matter your level with the EOS 1500D. With easy to use automatic shooting modes, large 24.1 MP sensor, Canon Camera Connect app integration and built-in feature guide, EOS 1500D is always ready to go.

  • Great for beginners
  • Learn how to capture fantastic shots from day one on your new EOS 1500D with its in-built feature guide and support from Canon's Camera Assist content series

  • Capture beautiful moments in stunning detail
  • The large 24.1 MP APS-C sized sensor delivers impressive image quality, coupled with advanced Canon lens technology for images with beautiful background blur
﻿ Upload to your social channels quickly and easily

Get social in seconds and post your latest photos on social media. Share your quality images by pairing the Canon Camera Connect app to your EOS 1500D

  • Get in those group shots
  • Connect with the Camera Connect app and use your smartphone as a remote control for camera settings with remote shooting so you can capture the perfect group portrait.

  • One touch connection with NFC
  • Getting connected is as easy as touching the camera and NFC compatible phone together. This will load the Canon Camera Connect app and you're good to go.


Features
  • See captured pictures and videos on 16:9 screen
  • Share the images from your camera to your phone effortlessly via built-in Wi-Fi
  • Impressive 3.1x zoom to lets you bring and feel everything closer
  • Focus on your object quickly even if the object starts moving
  • Take spectacular and high-resolution still images with 24.1 Megapixel camera
  • Record video with true lifelike colours and exceptionally sharp detail with the HD Movie Mode feature
  • 7.6 cm (3") LCD screen offers convenient display while you are shooting or viewing your photos
