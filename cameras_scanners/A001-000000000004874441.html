Description
Give your office powerful scan speed and accuracy with this powerful scanner. Built-in software and an 80-page automatic document feeder help you save time. Fast speeds allow you to scan at up to 65 pages per minute. Capture entire documents with one-pass duplex scanning up to 130 images per minute. EveryPage allows you to capture a wide range of documents, even stacks of mixed media sizes and types. Load up to 80 pages into the automatic document feeder for fast, unattended scanning. Scan images directly into applications with included and full-featured TWAIN and ISIS. Easily transfer scans into editable text, searchable PDF files and more file types, using built-in OCR. Create one-button, custom settings for recurring scan jobs and quickly make selections using the LCD control panel. Capture and organize documents, business cards and other file types with feature-rich software.
